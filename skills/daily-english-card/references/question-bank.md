# 英语每日三题题库（人教版七年级下）

## Unit 1-2 词形转换题库

### 1. health → healthy
```
The food here is very _____ (health). We should eat more vegetables.
答案：healthy（形容词作表语）
```

### 2. child → children
```
There are many _____ (child) in the playground. They are playing happily.
答案：children（复数名词）
```

### 3. real → really
```
It is _____ (real) important to exercise every day. I _____ love running. (副词修饰形容词/动词)
答案：really; really
```

### 4. exercise → exercises / exercising
```
My father _____ (exercise) every morning. That's his good _____ (exercise).
答案：exercises; exercise（动词三单/名词）
```

### 5. difference → different
```
The students are very _____ (difference) in their abilities. Everyone is _____.
答案：different; different（形容词）
```

### 6. mine → ours
```
This book is _____ (I). That one is _____. (名词性物主代词)
答案：mine; yours（需确认原文）
```

### 7. belong → belongs
```
The tennis match _____ (belong) to the school sports meeting. It _____ to Tom.
答案：belongs; doesn't belong（主谓一致）
```

### 8. nutrition → nutritious
```
The food is very _____ (nutrition). Eating _____ food keeps us healthy.
答案：nutritious; nutritious（形容词）
```

### 9. important → importance
```
It is _____ (important) to have a balanced diet. We should know the _____ of healthy eating.
答案：important; importance（名词形式）
```

### 10. health → healthy → healthily
```
Staying _____ (health) is very important. We should eat _____ (health).
答案：healthy; healthily（形容词/副词）
```

## Unit 3-4 运动/饮食话题

### 完形填空示例：Healthy Lifestyle

```
Jack is a Grade 7 student. He _____ (1) very healthy. Every day, he exercises for _____ (2) hour. He likes playing _____ (3) best.

He eats lots of _____ (4) and vegetables. He drinks milk every morning. He _____ (5) eats junk food.

What about his lifestyle? He usually _____ (6) at 6:30 and sleeps at 9:30. He thinks good habits are _____ (7) for us.

1. A. is  B. are  C. be  D. being
2. A. a  B. an  C. the  D. /
3. A. volleyball  B. basketball  C. tennis  D. swim
4. A. fruit  B. fruits  C. meat  D. bread
5. A. usually  B. never  C. always  D. sometimes
6. A. gets up  B. gets off  C. gets on  D. gets to
7. A. bad  B. good  C. terrible  D. terrible

答案：1-7 A B A B B A B
```

### 阅读理解示例：Sports

```
Jack plays basketball every day. He practices for one hour after school. His favorite player is Yao Ming. 

Jack's basketball team has a game this Saturday. They will play against the team from No. 2 Middle School. Jack is very excited because it's his first game.

1. How many hours does Jack practice basketball every day?
   A. One hour  B. Two hours  C. Three hours  D. Half an hour

2. Who is Jack's favorite player?
   A. Kobe  B. James  C. Yao Ming  D. Jordan

3. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Jack's daily life  B. Jack's basketball hobby  C. Jack's school  D. Jack's team
```

## 匹配题模板

### 对话匹配：Sports and Hobbies

**A. Hello! What's your favorite sport?**
**B. Me? I don't like soccer. How about you?**
**C. Because it's very interesting.**
**D. When do you have PE?**
**E. Why do you like basketball?**
**F. That sounds great!**

1. A: Hello, Bob! What's your favorite sport? → _____
2. A: Me? I don't like soccer. How about you? → _____
3. A: Because it's very interesting. → _____
4. A: Mr Wang is my basketball teacher. He's great fun. → _____
5. A: That sounds great! → _____

答案：1-B, 2-E, 3-C, 4-D, 5-F
